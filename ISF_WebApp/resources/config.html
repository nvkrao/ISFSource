Suggested Machine Configuration for Client Machines
The capacity of the user’s machine makes a significant difference in the performance of InscriptiFact’s many client functions.

Suggested minimum configurations for using InscriptiFact include:
  • 1.5 GHz machine,
  • 2Gb available memory,
  • 32Mb video ram.

    • InscriptiFact may be viewed at screen resolutions of 1280 x 1024, 1024 x 768, or 1400 x 1200. Some graphical anomalies may be apparent with other resolutions.
